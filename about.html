About Us

Made by hand, since day one

MD Cakes is a home-grown bakery on Old Road, Kopay Irupalai — every cake baked to order, not off a shelf.

Our Beginning

A small kitchen, a lot of trial batches

MD Cakes began in a home kitchen with one mixer, a handful of recipes, and a lot of patience. What started as cakes for family and friends slowly grew into orders from the wider Kopay community — one birthday, one wedding, one quiet Sunday treat at a time.

We still bake the same way we did on day one: measured by hand, decorated to order, and made fresh close to your collection date. No shelf cakes, no shortcuts.

— The MD Cakes kitchen

What We Believe

Three things we never skip

Baked fresh, always

Every cake is baked close to your pickup date — never frozen, never pre-made.

Made to your order

Flavour, size and design — every cake is built around what you actually asked for.

Honest ingredients

Good butter, real flavouring, and no unnecessary shortcuts in the mix.
